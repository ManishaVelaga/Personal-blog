GLOBAL IMMERSION PROGRAM AT UTP

My experience in Malaysia, Jan 30, 2023

The Global Immersion Program at Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS, Malaysia was a valuable learning experience that provided me with short courses in various fields such as technical reporting, finance management, critical thinking skills, data analysis, project management, and startup fundamentals.
These courses helped me enhance my skills and gain practical knowledge in respective areas.
During the program, I also had the opportunity to visit several popular tourist destinations in Malaysia, including Ipoh, Penang, Batu Caves, and KLCC,Petronas Towers. These visits provided a glimpse into the cultural richness and diversity of Malaysia, and allowed me to experience the local customs, traditions, and way of life.
Ipoh, located in the state of Perak, was known for its stunning colonial architecture, scenic parks, and delicious food. I was particularly impressed by the city's Old Town, which was filled with beautifully restored buildings from the colonial era. The street art and murals were also a highlight, as they added a vibrant and colorful touch to the city's historic streets.
Visiting the Batu Caves in Malaysia was a unique and awe-inspiring experience. Climbing the steep staircase to reach the Temple Cave was a bit of a workout, but the view from the top was worth it. From the entrance, I could see the lush greenery of the surrounding hills and the cityscape in the distance.
I had the opportunity to visit Petronas Towers, which are the tallest twin towers in the world.The visit to the Petronas Towers was a memorable experience. The sleek and modern design of the buildings, with their gleaming steel and glass fa√ßade, was truly awe-inspiring.
